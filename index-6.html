ABOUT US

Castle & Castle Law Firm is a legal practice based in Lagos, Nigeria,it has has a team of dedicated attorneys, paralegals, and support staff who are committed to providing personalized, high-quality legal representation to each of their clients. The firm has a proven track record of success, having secured millions of dollars in settlements and verdicts for their clients. The firm was founded in 2007 by attorneys Jason Dike and Benjamin Akinyemi.


Castle Law Firm prides itself on its commitment to client service and communication. The firm's attorneys are always available to answer questions and provide guidance throughout the legal process, and they work tirelessly to ensure that their clients receive the compensation they deserve.


Overall, Castle & Castle Law Firm is a highly respected legal practice that has earned a reputation for its dedication to its clients, its commitment to justice, and its contributions to the community.
